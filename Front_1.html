About Adventurous Kate

Hi! I’m Kate. I teach women how to travel the world safely — and I’m here to help you have the best trip ever. I quit my job to travel the world alone in 2010. 12 years later, I’ve traveled to 83 countries and all seven continents! Oh, and I never went back to a “real” job. Whether you need guidance for your first solo trip or you’re a seasoned traveler looking for destination inspiration, you’ve come to the right place!
